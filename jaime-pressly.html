Jaime Pressly is an American actress, fashion designer, and model who is best known for portraying roles such as Joy Turner in My Name Is Earl, Jill Kendall in Mom, Priscilla in Not Another Teen Movie, Tina Armstrong in DOA: Dead or Alive, and Audrey Griffin in Jack & Jill. She has also appeared in a variety of other TV shows and films such as Poison Ivy: The New Seduction, 100 Girls, Inferno, Demon Island, I Love You, Man, Austin Found, Bachelor Party Vegas, Mortal Kombat: Conquest, Push, The Twilight Zone, Charmed, Happy Family, Rules of Engagement, I Hate My Teenage Daughter, Two and a Half Men, BoJack Horseman, Finders Keepers, and many more. For her acting skills, she has been honored with numerous awards such as the Gold Derby Award and Primetime Emmy Award.
