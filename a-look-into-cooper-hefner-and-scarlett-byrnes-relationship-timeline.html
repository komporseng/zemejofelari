A Look Into Cooper Hefner And Scarlett Byrnes Relationship Timeline

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 8, 2024

Cooper Hefner and Scarlett Byrne have just welcomed twins into their family. On March 29, she revealed the news on Instagram, posting photos of herself and her husband embracing their children, as well as a close-up of their two girls. She penned:

“This weekend, Cooper and I welcomed our daughters, Marigold Adele Hefner and Blossom Pearl Hefner, into the world, and my heart swelled twice as big.” With our three girls, our house is full of love, and we couldn’t be happier.”

On her Instagram story, the Harry Potter actress posted a shot of her husband holding the two newborns in their carriers. Cooper also announced the news on Instagram, with the same photos and wording as his wife.

“Scarlett and I are pleased to report that on Saturday, March 26th, we welcomed Marigold Adele Hefner and Blossom Pearl Hefner into the world.” We’re settling back in at home today. How fortunate we are to have been given their lives. How fortunate we are to have been given this affection.”

Scarlett Byrne’s character in Harry Potter

Scarlett Byrne has portrayed Pansy Parkinson in the final three Harry Potter films: Half-Blood Prince, Deathly Hallows Part 1, and Part 2. In Harry Potter’s year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, she was a witch. She was born into the Parkinson’s family and was a member of the Sacred Twenty-Eight. She was put into Slytherin House and achieved perfection in her fifth year.

Pansy subsequently joined the short-lived Inquisitorial Squad. On May 2, 1998, during the last battle of the Second Wizarding War at Hogwarts, she argued in support of handing over Harry Potter to Lord Voldemort. In Harry’s defense, most of her classmates waved their wands at her. Pansy is thought to have survived the Great Wizarding War after leaving the Great Hall with the other Slytherins before the Battle of Hogwarts and did not return to battle. She had a falling out with her erstwhile buddy Draco Malfoy sometime after the war. Katherine Nicholson played a role in the Philosopher’s Stone and Chamber of Secrets, Genevieve Gaunt in Prisoner of Azkaban, Charlotte Richie in Goblet of Fire, and Lauren Shotton in Order of the Phoenix.

A look Into Cooper Hefner and Scarlett Byrne’s dating history

Cooper Hefner and Scarlett Byrne tied the knot in August of 2015. They announced their engagement in November 2019. On August 24, 2020, Byrne will give birth to her first child. Cooper stated that he and his wife were expecting twins in November 2021, and he captioned a picture of himself, his wife, and their daughter:

“This spring, Scarlett and I will be welcoming two more family members.” We are overjoyed as we await the birth of our twins. “To everyone, a very Happy Thanksgiving!”
