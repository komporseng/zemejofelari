Inside The Life Of Oona Laurence - Bio &amp; Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 21, 2024

Oona Laurence is an actress with more than a decade of experience in the American film industry. Besides her career as an actor, she is also a professional model. 

She got fame and recognization after portraying the role of Matilda Wormwood in Matilda on Broadway, a musical show based on1988 children's novel. 

She was born on August 1, 2002, in New York City, New York, USA. In addition, Laurence's star sign is Leo, and she holds American nationality.

Awards & Nominations

What We Found actress Laurence has been able to achieve many awards at a very young age. She has been awarded for her notable acting and role in many films. In 2014, she got her first "Technical Award" for the role she portrayed in Penny Dreadful. 

After that, in 2017, she got two awards. One was the "Young Artist Award " for the Best Performance in a Feature Film, and the second was the "Halfway Award" for Best Ensemble Cast.

Apart from this, Laurence is nominated for many awards. Some of them are the "Jury Award," "WAFCA Award," and "Young Entertainer Award."
