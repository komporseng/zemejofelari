JoJo Siwa is an American actress, singer, dancer, and social media personality. As of 2022, JoJo Siwa’s net worth is $20 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing, dancing, and acting career. She is known for portraying Victoria Martin in the comedy film “Blurt” (2018) and for her appearance in the TV reality show “Dance Moms” (2011) along with her mother, Jessalynn Siwa.

In 2016, she released her debut single “Boomerang”, written by Griffith Frank, JoJo Siwa, Jordan Richman and Heather Miley. The single was also certified Gold and Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). She posts regular videos of her daily life on her YouTube channel, “Its JoJo Siwa”. In 2020, she listed among the 100 most influential people in the world.

Career

JoJo Siwa started her career as a dancer and participated in the TV reality show Abby’s Ultimate Dance Competition and emerged as the top finalists in the show. After that, she has appeared in the TV reality show Dance Moms with her mother.

In 2016, she released her debut single titled Boomerang which focused on the subject of online bullying. She then released other singles like I Can Make You Dance, Kid in a Candy Store, Hold The Drama, NonStop, Only Getting Better, and several others. She also worked in popular movies like Blurt and The Angry Birds Movie 2.

She also made her appearance in various popular TV series like Good Day New York, Make it Pop, School of Rock, JoJo’s Dream Birthday, All That, The Substitute, The Masked Singer, and many others.

She released extended plays like D.R.E.A.M The Music, Celebrate, and JoJo’s Rockin’ Christmas. She also uploads videos on her YouTube channel regularly.

Awards

She has won the Industry Dance Awards for Favorite Dancer 17 & Under in 2015 and Reality Television Awards for Most Heartfelt Moment in the year 2016.

She has won the Nickelodeon Kid’s Choice Awards back to back for four years in different categories like Favorite Viral Music Artist, Favorite Musical YouTube Creator, Favorite TV Host, and Favorite Social Music Star.
