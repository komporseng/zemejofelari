Who is Michael Jordan's new wife Yvette Prieto?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Michael Jordan, often regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, found love with Yvette Prieto after his divorce from his first wife, Juanita Vanoy.

Their relationship, marriage, family life, and the privacy they maintain despite their public profiles have piqued the curiosity of many.

Yvette Prieto's Background

Yvette Prieto, a Cuban-born model, grew up in Miami and had a successful career as a model, working for renowned designers such as Alexander Wang. Before meeting Michael Jordan, she dated Julio Iglesias Jr., the brother of the famous singer Enrique Iglesias.

In 2009, Michael and Yvette moved in together in a 5,500-square-foot home in Miami, signifying the beginning of their journey together.

The Love Story

The love story of Michael Jordan and Yvette Prieto is nothing short of a fairytale. They first met in 2007 on the dancefloor at a club in Miami, where they instantly connected.

A little over a year after their meeting, in 2009, they decided to move in together, further solidifying their relationship. On Christmas Eve in 2011, Michael Jordan proposed to Yvette, marking a significant milestone in their love story.

The Wedding

On April 27, 2013, Michael Jordan and Yvette Prieto tied the knot in a lavish, star-studded, and secret ceremony in front of 300 of their closest family and friends. 

The wedding, held at the Episcopal Church of Bethesda-by-the-Sea, was followed by a reception at Bear's Club in Jupiter, Florida. The celebration of love came with an estimated final price tag of around $10 million, reflecting the grandeur of the event.

Family Life

In February 2014, the couple welcomed their twin daughters, Victoria and Ysabel, into the world. Despite their public profiles, Michael and Yvette have chosen to keep their children largely out of the spotlight, respecting their privacy.

It's evident that family holds a special place in their hearts, and they have carefully guarded their family life from the public eye.

Privacy and Security

Despite being a private couple, the public nature of Michael Jordan's celebrity demanded extra security for the couple. Jordan reportedly hired a private security team to accompany them on overseas trips and used code names to ensure their privacy and safety.

Yvette's secret code name was Harmony, while Jordan went by Yahweh. This speaks volumes about the lengths they went to in order to maintain their privacy.

Financial Arrangements

Unlike his previous marriage with Juanita Vanoy, Michael Jordan and Yvette Prieto signed a prenuptial agreement. According to a legal document presented by the Daily Mail, each will keep whatever assets they bring into the marriage.

In the event of a divorce, Yvette will receive $1 million for every year they're married. Furthermore, if the couple splits up after 10 years, Yvette will receive $5 million for every year they stayed together, highlighting the financial arrangements they have in place.

Yvette Prieto's love story with Michael Jordan, their grand wedding, and their decision to keep their family life private have captivated the public's interest.

Despite being married to one of the most famous athletes in history, Yvette has maintained a sense of privacy and normalcy in her life.

Their marriage and family life reflect a beautiful balance of love, privacy, and commitment, making them an inspiring couple to many.
