Wrestling Stars Juice Robinson And Toni Storm Are Officially Dating

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 27, 2024

The two wrestling stars Toni Storm and Juice Robinson have now found their love. The NXT UK star Toni Storm and NJPW star, Juice Robinson, made their relationship public on 5 June 2020 via Instagram.

Toni shared a romantic picture on her Instagram where Juice can be seen holding her on his arms and kissing as well. The picture was snapped by the AEW star Brie Priestly. And many other wrestlers came forth with their congratulating messages down the comment section.

As far as the relationship between Toni Storm and Juice Robinson is concerned, they have just confirmed the sparkles of their romance they showcased back in a month.

Previously, Toni had hinted her dating life with Juice as she posted a picture of them with a heart emojis. And the speculation came true.

(©: Instagram/Toni Storm)

With the recent Instagram post, Juice Robinson and Toni Storm have opened up their bond as boyfriend and girlfriend. Congratulations to the couple. 

Who Is Juice Robinson?

Juice Robinson is an American wrestler who plays his career from the New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW). He was born as Jūsu Robinson on 10 April 1989. However, he goes by his wrestling name, Juice Robinson.

Juice Robinson's career trails his works with WWE for a year from 2011 to 2012. He wrestled for the development territories of WWE in Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW) and later in NXT (2012-15) under the ring name CJ Parker. He also won a couple of FCW tag team championship alongside Jason Jordan and Donny Marlow.

Besides that, he made a special appearance as Red Death Mask and joined Ring of Honor (ROH) as the leader of the Lifeblood stable.

Talking about his current career specifications, he is a two-time IWGP United States Champion and a one-time IWGP Tag Team Champion alongside David Finlay. In 2019, he won the World Tag League with David Finlay.

As of 2020, his wrestling career summarizes twelve years of fighting inside rings. Juice, who trained at Truth Martini's House of Truth wrestling school and made his professional debut in 2008, is also World Tag Team Champions.
