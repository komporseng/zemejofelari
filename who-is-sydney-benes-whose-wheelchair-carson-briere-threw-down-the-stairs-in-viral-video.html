Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 3, 2024

Sydney Benes, a disabled woman from Erie, Pennsylvania, has criticised Carson Briere in public after he was caught on camera video pushing her empty wheelchair down a flight of stairs at Sullivan’s Irish Pub

Briere used to play hockey at Mercyhurst University. After the incident, Briere and two other people who were involved were banned from all team activities by Mercyhurst. Briere is the son of former NHL player Daniel Briere.

At the time of the accident, Sydney was not in the wheelchair.

His +5 +/- rating was second best on the team, and his four power play goals tied for the most on the team. He put up 17 more blocks.

On October 15 against Holy Cross, he scored a season-high four goals. From 2022 to 2023, he played 30 games for the Lakers and started 10 of them. He scored 5 goals and had 8 assists during that time. During the season, Briere had 41 shots on goal and 12 blocks.
