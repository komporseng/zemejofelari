Celebration as Arsenals Mesut Ozil and wife, Amine, welcome their first child together a baby

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Exciting news from Arsenal superstar Mesut Ozil and Amine Ozil as they welcome their first child together – a baby girl.

The former Real Madrid star, Mesut, took to social media on Monday night to share the heartwarming announcement, accompanying it with a lovely photo of himself and his wife with their newborn, named Eda. 

He captioned the image with these words: “Thank God our daughter “Eda” was born healthy. God willing us to be a good son to his environment and all humanity.”
