Sophia Lucia is an American famous dancer, actress as well as a singer. Sophia Lucia gained fame as a reality TV fan for her role in the Lifetime series Dance Moms.

Early life

Sophia Lucia is 21 years old. She was born September 7, 2002, in San Diego, California, under the astrological sign of Virgo. Her father’s name is Mike Lucia and her mother’s name is Jackie Lucia. She belongs to the American nationality and Caucasian ethnicity background. Currently, she lives in Los Angeles, California, United States.

Likewise, she also has a brother named Vin “Vinny” Lucia. She is currently a high school student and is homeschooled. From a very young age, she started dancing. Her cousin Kristen Hibbs runs a dancing academy “San Deigo Centre” and introduced her to the dance.
