Who is Michael Jordan?

Michael Jordan is widely regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time. He is known for his amazing skills, competitive spirit, and clutch performances on the court.

Jordan retired from basketball three times, each time making headlines and shocking fans. He first retired in 1993, after winning his first three-peat, to pursue a career in baseball. He returned to the NBA in 1995, and won another three-peat with the Bulls. He retired again in 1998, after hitting the game-winning shot in the NBA Finals. Yes, Michael Jordan is alive still. He is 61 years old as of 2023, and lives in Florida with his wife and children. He occasionally makes public appearances and remains an influential figure in the basketball world.

How to avoid falling for death hoaxes?

Death hoaxes are not new, but they can be very convincing and upsetting. Here are some tips on how to avoid falling for them:

  • Check the source. Is it a reputable news outlet or a verified social media account? If not, be skeptical and do not trust it blindly.
  • Check the date. Is it recent or old? Sometimes, old news or hoaxes are recycled and circulated again to create confusion and panic.
  • Check the facts. Is there any evidence or confirmation from reliable sources? If not, do not believe it without further investigation.
  • Check the motive. Why would someone spread such a rumor? Is it for money, fame, or mischief? Do not give them what they want by sharing or liking their posts or videos.
