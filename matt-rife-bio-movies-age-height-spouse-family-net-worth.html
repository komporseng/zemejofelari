Matt Rife Biography

Matthew Steven Rife is a famous American comedian and actor who is well-known for his self-produced comedy specials such as Only Fans, Matthew Steven Rife, and Walking Red Flag. He is one of the youngest regular male performers at the World Famous Laugh Factory. Moreover, Rife popularity also increased when he appeared in MTV’s popular show ‘Wild N Out’ season seven and become the youngest cast member on the show. Throughout his career, he has managed to perform with renowned comedians such as Finesse Mitchell, Mike Epps, Erik Griffin, and Dane Cook among others.

Matt was born in Columbus, Ohio, and brought up in North Lewisburg, where he attended his high school study. He become interested in comedy at the age of 14 and did his first comedy show at the age of 15 at a school competition. Since then he has toured around the world performing with some of the biggest names in comedy such as Finesse Mitchell, Dane Cook, Mike Epps, Ralphie May, and Deray Davis among others.

Currently, he is one of the youngest male regular performers at the World Famous Laugh Factory. Rife is well recognized for his self-produced comedy specials such as Only Fans, Matthew Steven Rife, and Walking Red Flag. Apart from that, he has also participated in shows like Bring the Funny, where he advanced to the Semi-Final Showcase but was eliminated in week 8. Prior to working as a comedy, Matt has also starred in some films such as Room 236, Sophomore Year, Black Pumpkin, The Debt, and The Elevator among others.

In 2022, Matt starred in films including North of the 10 and Wolf Moutain. In addition, he has over 15 million followers on Tik Tok as of June 2023.

Matt Rife Age

How old is Matt Rife? He is 27 years old as of 2023. Matt was born on September 10, 1995, in Columbus, Ohio, United States. In addition, he celebrates his birthday on September 10 every year.

Matt Rife Height/ Weight

Rife stands at an approximate height of 6 feet and weighs around 80 kg. He is a handsome guy with brown hair and blue eyes which make him attractive.

Matt Rife Spouse

Is Rife Matt married or dating? He is now single and is not dating anyone at the moment. However, in the past, he was in a relationship with English actress Kate Beckinsale. The couple were together for one year before they parted ways. In 2021, during his special comedy show, he mentioned that he has clinical depression and anxiety disorder.

Matt Rife Family

Matt is the son of the late Micheal Eric Gutzke and his mom April Rife Chilton. April is married to Jason Sievers who is the step-father of Matt. In addition, he also has four siblings, three older stepsisters, and one younger half-sister. Moreover, his father died by committing suicide when Rife was 12 months old.

Matt Rife’s Net Worth

How much does Matt Rife earn? He has accumulated an approximate net worth of between $ 1.5 million to $ 5 million dollars. He has acquired his fortune from his successful career as an actor and comedian.
