How much does Kenny Smith make on TNT?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Kenny Smith, a former NBA player turned television basketball analyst, has built a successful career in the broadcasting industry, particularly with his work on TNT's "Inside the NBA." 

Base Salary and Contract Extension

Kenny Smith's primary source of income from his work on TNT comes in the form of a substantial base salary. As of 2024, Smith receives an impressive annual salary of $4 million.

This notable figure reflects the value of his contributions as a basketball analyst for TNT, particularly as a co-host of the highly popular "Inside the NBA" program alongside Ernie Johnson Jr., Charles Barkley, and Shaquille O'Neal. 

Kenny Smith's financial outlook appears promising, as he recently signed a long-term contract extension with Turner Sports, indicating a continued and lucrative partnership that is likely to further bolster his net worth.
