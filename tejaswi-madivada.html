Biography

Tejaswi Madivada was born in Hyderabad, India on July 3, 1991. Former pageant contestant turned film star who debuted in the family drama Seethamma Vaakitlo Sirimalle Chettu. She has since gone on to lead Telugu films like Manam, Heart Attack, and Anukshanam. In 2015, she appeared in the film Kerintha alongside Sri Divya. She studied mass communication and journalism at St Francis College for Women. She later worked as a part-time dance instructor.
