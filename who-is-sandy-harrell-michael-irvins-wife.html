Sandy Harrell Became Michael Irvin’s Wife in the Early ’90s

Details of exactly how they met or how long they dated before they tied the knot are things the couple apparently prefer to keep to themselves. With the worst of the storm behind them and to prove her eternal love and fidelity to Michael, she even gave him a car, a Bentley Mulsanne, in 2016 as a gift for his 50th birthday.
