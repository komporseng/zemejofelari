Career

Jessica is certainly retired from her professional career as a footballer. This personality has joined multiple clubs and played in various leagues. She began her career in Djurgardens IF and moved to Hammarby IF DFF.

This former player represented the Sweden women’s national football team at the 2008 Olympic Football Tournament, the 2009 UEFA Women’s Championship, and the 2011 FIFA Women’s World Cup.

Moreover, at the club level, she has played for Damallsvenskan teams Djurgårdens IF, Linköpings FC, Hammarby IF DFF, and Kopparbergs/Göteborg FC.

Further, she has also played for 1. FFC Frankfurt of Germany and Sky Blue FC of the USA. Jessica was included in coach Thomas Dennerby’s squad for the 2008 Olympic Games.

She played in 4 games in China and retained her place in the national squad for UEFA Women’s Euro 2009.
