Born on May 23, 1947, in Rochester to Joseph and Thelma Graham, Tom leaves behind a legacy of warmth, kindness, and cherished memories. As we reflect on his life, we celebrate the man who touched the hearts of those around him.

Thomas, or Tom as he was affectionately known, spent his formative years in the embrace of his loving family. His roots in Rochester provided the foundation for a life that would later unfold in Wadena. Family was of utmost importance to Tom, and his bond with his siblings was a testament to the strong ties that connected them. Sadly, he was preceded in death by his brother, George. Nevertheless, his memory lives on through the stories and shared experiences with his surviving siblings—Leonard, Joe, and Marsha.
