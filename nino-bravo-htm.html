Nino Bravo's About

[✎]

Nino Bravo was born on August 3, 1944 (age 28) in Spain. He is a Celebrity Pop Singer. Spanish-born pop singer who released a successful 1972 album entitled Libre. Due to its title, the album was banned by Fidel Castro (World Leader) and many other Latin American dictators. According to numerology, Nino Bravo's Life Path Number is 2. His genres are Baroque pop,Latin ballad. His successful albums are Te quiero, te quiero, Un beso y una flor, Mi tierra. His popular songs are Un Beso y una FlorUn beso y una flor · 1972, LibreMi tierra · 1972 and NoeliaUn beso y una flor · 1972. He died on April 16, 1973, Villarrubio, Spain.
