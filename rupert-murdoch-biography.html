Rupert Murdoch Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 4, 2024

Who is Rupert Murdoch?

Rupert Murdoch is an American media tycoon.

He is associated with several local and international news media such as The Sun, The Times, Fox News, and more.

Rupert Murdoch- Age, Parents, Ethnicity

He was born Rupert Keith Murdoch on 11 March 1931 in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

His father and his mother’s names were Sir Keith Murdoch and Dame Elisabeth Murdoch respectively.

His mom passed away in 2012 while his dad kicked the bucket in 1952.

Rupert’s ethnic background is of English-Irish-Scottish.

Education

Talking about academics, he achieved his BA degree from Worcester College, Oxford.

Also from the same institute, he passed out with a Master’s degree in Arts.

Rupert Murdoch- Career Timeline

Rupert Murdoch is at present serving News Corp as its Executive chairman.

He joined the team in 2013. In addition, he is also the Chairman of Fox Corporation since 2019.

He has earned an estimated net worth of $24 Billion in 2022.

Real Estates

This media mogul has several properties. He owns a property of 12,000-square-foot which he bought at $23M in 2011.

This house is located on the Upper East Side of New York.

He also spent around $28.8 million to buy a 16-acre vineyard and a building in Bel-Air.

Then after a year in 2104, he bought a 6,850-square-foot penthouse for $57.25 million.
