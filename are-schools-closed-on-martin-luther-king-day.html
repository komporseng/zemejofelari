Are schools closed on Martin Luther King Day 2022?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

In the United States of America, Martin Luther King Day is a federal holiday in the United States marking the birthday of Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther King Day is annually observed on the third Monday of January each year and in 2022, the day falls on Monday, January 17, 2022.

The day is also referred to by some people as MLK Day, King Day, Reverend Dr. Martin Luther, and King Jr. Day.

Martin Luther King is celebrated all over the world and the black community for his activism who fought for the civil rights of the negros. He was assassinated in 1968

Are schools closed on Martin Luther King Day 2022?

In the United States of America, schools, colleges, as well as universities across the country are closed for the MLK Day holiday. The schools are scheduled to resume on Tuesday, January 18th, 2022.
